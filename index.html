About FiPARSo

Accelerating discovery through interdisciplinary collaboration.

FiPARSo is an applied research initiative based at IIT Hyderabad, dedicated to bridging the gap between molecular biology and computational intelligence. We develop machine learning frameworks designed specifically to decode complex biological datasets, optimizing scientific discovery pipelines from genome to model architecture.

Our methodology emphasizes rigorous data analysis, validating computational hypotheses through close alignment with experimental research paradigms to establish scalable frameworks for translational medicine.

Fifth Paradigm Lab

FIFTH PARADIGM LAB

Fifth Paradigm Lab is where FiPARSo develops the researchers, ideas, and methods behind its work. It exists to grow scientific thinking and genuine interdisciplinary collaboration — bringing biology, computation, and clinical insight together so that early hypotheses can mature into applied, real-world innovation. The Lab is deliberately open by design: every framework built here is meant to be tested, shared, and carried forward alongside academic and industry partners, not developed in isolation.
